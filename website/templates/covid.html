COVID-19

Following all CDC recommendations to protect you and minimize the threat of COVID-19

Keeping You Safe

Every piece of equipment used during training sessions is fully sanitized. This is above and beyond any standard of a brick-and-mortar gym as their high volume practically restricts their ability to protect their customers to this degree. I take pride in my ability to protect my clients and minimize the risk of COVID-19 by using antiseptics including, but not limited to, isopropyl alcohol, bleach, and Clorox commercial disinfecting wipes.

Doing Our Part

Everything is done to maximize the safety and comfort of my clients. If you have any particular requests, I am more than happy to consider them.

  • All equipment is completely sanitized after each session
  • Body-weight excercises can be substituted for those concerned with surface transmission
  • No common spaces are necessary
  • Safety remains the top priority
  • Sessions can be postponed or cancelled if you are in any way feeling unwell
  • Mobile Firehouse Fitness started from the COVID-19 pandemic with safety and comfort as the top priorities
