COVID-19

Following all CDC recommendations to protect you and minimize the threat of COVID-19

Keeping You Safe

I make an effort to fully sanitize each and every piece of equipment used during training sessions. This is above and beyond any standard of a brick-and-mortar gym as their high volume practically restricts their ability to protect their customers to this degree. I take pride in my ability to protect my clients and minimize the risk of COVID-19 by using antiseptics including but not limited to isopropyl alcohol, bleach, and Clorox commercial disinfecting wipes.

Doing Our Part

I do everything I can to maximize the safety and comfort of my clients. If you have any particular requests in addition to my standard precautions, I am more than happy to use them.

  • All equipment is completely sanitized after each session.
  • Body-weight excercises can be substituted for those concerned with surface transmission
  • No common spaces are necessary
  • Safety remains the top priority
  • All sessions can be postponed or cancelled if you are in any way feeling unwell
  • Mobile Firehouse Fitness started from the COVID-19 pandemic with safety and comfort as the top priorities
