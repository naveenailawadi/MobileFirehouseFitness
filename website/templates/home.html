About Me - Oliver James

I’ve always had a passion for fitness and was actively involved in sports throughout my entire life, including football, basketball and track. I received my certification for personal training through Internal Sports Sciences Association (ISSA) in June 2016.

  • Focused on accountability across the board
  • Working with clients to provide the safest, cleanest environment possible
  • Doing my best to make personal and group training as easy and accessible as possible

Mobile Firehouse Fitness is one of the most convenient, reliable, mobile gym services in the Lehigh Valley. I focus on bringing the gym to you on your time to fit your schedule as best possible.

Training Philosophy

My philosophy focuses on lifelong learning as I work towards improving the well-being of my clients by empowering them the knowledge, support, skills, guidance and resources to assist and inspire them on their journey to a healthier life. Fitness is here to be enjoyed. Fitness provides us with the ability to enjoy a healthy active lifestyle. The best part about it is that you get to choose your own fitness journey. I am a certified personal trainer that specializes in one-on-one training as well as group classes that are creative and fun with high energy levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are your prices?

    Prices vary based on times, days, and traveling distances.

  • How did this get started?

    I used to own a studio gym until late 2019. Upon the rise of the COVID-19, I began to innovate and mobilized my services. This allowed me to create a more comfortable and reliable gym service for my clients.

  • Where are you located?

    I primarily travel within the Lehigh Valley but am available across eastern Pennsylvania. In addition, I hold weekly sessions at outdoor spaces across the valley.
